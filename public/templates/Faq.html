Selling A Car?


How much does it cost to use Redrive? Carista charges a one time flat fee of $199. That actually means $199, no hidden fees, no add-ons for something extra that we didn’t tell you about. Are there any additional fees? To reiterate the previous point, there are no additional fees. The one time fee of $199 covers the valuation by a skilled technician, a 150 point inspection conducted by an ASE certified mechanic, a car wash, stunning photo shoot modeling your car, the creation of your seller profile, and the integration of all the wonderful information about your soon to be sold vehicle. Who Inspects my Vehicle? One of our ASE certified mechanics. What happens if my vehicle does not pass the inspection? Sometimes a car doesn’t meet the Redrive standards, don’t take it personally, it wasn’t you, it was us. So, we refund you $90. We also provide the inspection details, and a discount that can be applied if you wish to have the issue corrected by one of our mechanic partners. Are there any restrictions once my car has been inspected? Yes. For the duration of the listing period you cannot drive more than 2,000 miles. Why do we do this? At Redrive we pride ourselves on reliability, so once an inspection has been completed we need to ensure the car stays in the same condition as it was when it was inspected. Also, additional mileage affects value, since we offer your vehicle at a flat rate, we need to make sure the value remains the same. Am I responsible for test drives? Yes. Carista will work with you and the buyer to set up a time for the test drive. Do I have to leave my couch to sell my car? No! Buying A Car?


Is Redrive a Dealership? No we are not a dealership, yuck. What do I do with my old car? If you car meets the Redrive standards, we would be happy to assist you in selling the car. If you have already bought through Redrive we offer a 15% discount on your listing. Can I test drive the car I want to buy? Absolutely. You can schedule a test drive with the seller once you log-in, or on the seller profile page. How do I know the car is reliable? Every car on Redrive has passed a 150 point inspection conducted by an ASE certified mechanic. This is a serious inspection and is similar to the inspection performed on certified pre-owned vehicles at many of the nation’s top dealerships How do I know I am getting a fair price? Redrive uses leading valuation technology to determine the price of your vehicle. We use well known sources such as Kelley’s Blue Book and Edmunds, as well as other factors including local market conditions, and similar cars recently sold. Our vehicles are sold for private party value, saving you the money the dealership tacks on. Is the price negotiable? Unfortunately, or maybe fortunately it is not. The price listed is the final price. It is the car’s actual value, not a penny more. Your ego doesn’t need to win a negotiation for you wallet to be happy! Does Carista Offer Financing? Yes. When you apply for financing on Carista we send your loan request to a network of leading automotive lenders who compete to get you the best loan possible.
